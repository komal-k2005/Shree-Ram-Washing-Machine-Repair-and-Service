About Shri Ram Enterprises

Serving Pune West families with pride. Discover our story, values, and commitment to superior technical craftsmanship.

Our Story & Vision

Formed as a local solution to the growing demand for trusted appliance repair in Baner, Shri Ram Enterprises has established a strong reputation as Pune West's go-to agency. We believe that home appliances are essential parts of modern daily routines, and a breakdown requires swift, neat, and highly professional repair services.

Our workshop, situated at Shop No. 4, Amar Serenity Condominium Complex, allows our technicians to deploy quickly to Baner, Balewadi, Aundh, Hinjewadi, Wakad, Pashan, and surrounding areas. We invest in high-end diagnostic tools, pressure gauges, and training programs to ensure our team stays at the absolute forefront of appliance servicing technology.

Our Core Values

Absolute Honesty: No inflated diagnostic fees or false parts swapping. We repair only what is broken.
Technical Competency: Continuous learning on microprocessor control boards and energy-efficient cooling loop systems.
Neat & Safe Work: Technicians ensure that your kitchen and utility floors are cleaned up post-servicing.
Customer Dedication: Quick call-backs, flexible appointment slots, and reliable post-repair warranty servicing.

Why We Believe in Repairing

We are firm believers in sustainability. Throwing away a washing machine or refrigerator just because a relay sensor, valve, or belt failed is both economically wasteful and environmentally harmful. Our repair services extend the operating life of your home investments by years, saving you money and reducing e-waste at the same time.
