Common Issues We Fix Daily

If you notice any of these symptoms in your machine, it's time to call in our experts:

  • Excessive Vibrating or Shaking: Often caused by broken dampers, worn out drum suspension springs, or unbalanced load distribution.
  • Drainage Failures: Caused by clogged drainage pumps, faulty impeller mechanisms, or blocked outlet pipes.
  • Spin Failures: The motor hums but the drum won't rotate. This points to capacitor wear, drive belt slippage, or motor brushes.
  • Water Leaking: Usually associated with torn door bellows/seal gaskets, faulty water inlet valves, or pipe punctures.
  • Error Codes on Display: Digital control board (PCB) programming glitches or faulty relay sensors that require testing.
