Expert Microwave Oven Repair Services

A microwave oven is a essential appliance for heating, baking, and quick food preparation in modern kitchens. Due to high-voltage circuitry inside, repairing a microwave requires professional equipment and safety measures. Our technicians at Shri Ram Enterprises are thoroughly trained in handling microwave radiation shielding, high-voltage capacitors, and micro-electronics diagnostics safely.

Common Issues We Troubleshoot

Here are the common faults our technicians repair on-site:

  • Not Heating: The microwave turns on and timer runs, but food stays cold. This points to a faulty magnetron unit, high-voltage diode, or burnt transformer.
  • Sparking Inside: Sparks appearing inside the chamber are often caused by a damaged mica sheet wave-guide cover, or exposed metal on the inner enamel walls.
  • Turntable Not Rotating: Food is heated unevenly because the glass tray won't spin. Caused by a burnt tray motor under the chassis or broken roller guide wheels.
  • Touchpad/Buttons Unresponsive: Membrane control switch panel damage or PCB logic board chips failing, requiring micro-soldering or panel swap.
  • Runs for 2-3 Seconds and Shuts Off: Indicative of door switch safety interlock failures or overheating safety sensors.

Safety-First Repair Workflow

Microwaves store massive, potentially lethal high-voltage electrical charges even when completely unplugged from wall power. Never attempt to open the casing of a microwave yourself. Our technicians carry specialized safety probes to discharge capacitors before testing components.
